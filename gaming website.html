About

What we do

We develop, publish and distribute multiplayer mobile games.
We reach over 400 million players each month, and our audience keeps growing.

Our History

Founded in 2001, we’ve successfully grown a huge global audience in over 195 countries and across six continents. We’ve also grown in size by recruiting brilliant people, working with, and acquiring studios worldwide to
help us fulfil our purpose of ‘unleashing the gamer in everyone’.

Our Hits

Our Miniclip franchises include 8 Ball Pool™, Subway Surfers™, Golf Battle™, Football Strike™, Carrom Pool™, and Agar.io™
which have generated more than 7 billion downloads.
We have a unique understanding of the digital games space, connecting more than 70 million daily active players worldwide.
